The Need

A significant number of pastors and christian leaders are considering leaving their ministry calling. While pastoral leadership is a wonderful privilege and responsibility, pastors are very real people too. Some of the challenges they face include:

  • Church conflict
  • Unrealistic congregational expectations
  • Discouragement
  • Financial challenges
  • Depression
  • Health issues
  • Marital issues
  • Etc..

    • Continue

    Leaders

    These Leaders need a safe and confidential place to be heard by those who understand these challenges. Other leaders are doing well in their current place of ministry, and we want to see them remain strong and encouraged as they lead His church.

    Vision

    Leader to Leader Is called to:

    Encourage - "Therefore encourage one another and build each other up, just as in fact you are doing." 1 Thess 5:11

    Inspire - "Think of ways to motivate one another to outbursts of love and good deeds." Heb 10:24

    Dream -"When dreams come true, there is life and joy." Prov 13:12b

    Our Story

    Norm and Corlynn Christofferson have been in pastoral ministry for the past 37 years. They have served in a number if ministry capacities and have experienced both the joys and challenges of ministry life facing their own crisis point in ministry, God has brought them to a place of healing and restoration. For a number of years they have carried a burden for leaders who are needing encouragement, and they have stepped alongside several leaders to strengthen and encourage them. With pastoral hearts they will be serving as Barnabas did in encouraging and inspiring leaders to dream again.
