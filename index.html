LEADER TO LEADER MINISTRIES

The Need

A significant number of pastors and christian leaders are considering leaving their ministry calling. While pastoral leadership is a wonderful privilege and responsibilty, pastors are very real people too. Some of the challenges they face include.

  • Church conflict
  • Unrealistic congregational expectations
  • Discouragement
  • Financial challenges
  • depression
  • Health issues
  • Etc..

    Our Story

    Norm and Corlynn Christofferson have been in pastorial ministry for the past 37 years. They have served in a number if ministry capacities and have experienced both the joys and challenges of ministry life Facing their own crisis poinmt in ministry, God has brought them to a palce of healing and restoration. For a number of years they have carried a burden for leaders who are needing encouragement, and they have stepped alongside several leaders to strength and encourage them. With pastoral hearts they will be serving as Barnabas did in encouraging and inspiring leaders to dream again.
