Our Story

Norm and Corlynn Christofferson have been in pastoral ministry for over 40 years. They have served in a number of ministry capacities and have experienced both the joys and challenges of ministry life. Facing their own crisis point in ministry, God has brought them to a place of healing and restoration. For a number of years they have carried a burden for leaders who are needing encouragement, and they have stepped alongside several leaders to strengthen and encourage them. With pastoral hearts they are serving as Barnabas did in encouraging and inspiring leaders to dream again. Along with Leader to Leader, Norm and Corlynn are busy as interim pasotors, traveling across Montana to come alongside churches who are looking for a new pastor.

Interim Pastoring

As they've been reaching out to leaders, unique doors have been opening. Interim pastoring is one of those areas. When a pastor moves on from a church after serving for some time, it can be a difficult season for a congregation to walk through. Several churches that are going through pastoral transition have had them come and serve as pastors, leading and guiding the church until a new pastor arrives. This ministry has become a significant part of Leader to Leader Ministries.

"Join us as we reach out to encourage and bless pastors and other Christian leaders. We ask you to commit to praying for your own pastor on a regular basis."

-Pastor Norm & Corlynn-
