Do you need help?

A significant number of pastors and christian leaders are considering leaving their ministry calling. While pastoral leadership is a wonderful privilege and responsibility, pastors are very real people too. Some of the challenges they face include:

  • Church conflict
  • Unrealistic congregational expectations
  • Discouragement
  • Financial challenges
  • Depression
  • Health issues
  • Marital issues
  • Etc..

    • We are are heavy burdened to help those in need. Leader to Leader is a safe and confidential place to be heard by those who understand these challenges. While some leaders are doing well in their current place of ministry, we want to see all remain strong and encouraged as they lead His church.
