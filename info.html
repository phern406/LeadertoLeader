LEADER TO LEADER MINISTRIES

"Encourage, Inspire, Dream."

How can we help?

A significant number of pastors and Christian leaders are considering leaving their ministry calling calling due to church conflict, unrealistic congregational expectations, discouragement, financial challenges, marital issues, depression, health issues, and more.

As every pastor and Christian leader knows, ministry is both a privilege and a challenge. It is an incredible honor to serve God by following the call He has placed on our lives to encourage and bless people. It is a challenge to work with fallen human beings who are walking through woundedness.

We are here to bless and encourage leaders who are in, or have been in, difficult times. We make regular contact by phone. We travel extensively to meet with leaders. We hold retreats periodically where worship, encouragement, and fun happens. Because of our generous partners, this ministry is offered to you at no charge.

If you as a leader need encouragement and a listening ear, or if you know of a leader in need, please contact us.
